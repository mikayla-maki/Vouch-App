Vouch

Recommendations from people you actually trust.

Vouch is a local-first app for sharing what's good — restaurants, books, experiences, anything worth vouching for — along real trust lines. Your words live on your devices, signed by you, shared only with the people you choose. No feeds, no ads, no algorithm deciding what you see.

Early days. This server is a relay: it holds signed, end-attributed claims for about a week so friends can sync without being online at the same moment, then lets them go. If you're connecting an app, you're already in the right place — this domain is the relay (wss://) too.
